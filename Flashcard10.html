Deep Dive: Value Over Volume is a critical component of the Excellence Mindset. In a world obsessed with activity metrics—calls made, emails sent, meetings attended—excellence focuses on impact created. It's not about making the most calls; it's about having the most impactful conversations. It's not about attending every meeting; it's about contributing meaningfully to the meetings that matter. It's not about being the busiest; it's about being the most effective. This mindset shift is difficult in organizations that measure only volume, but it's essential for sustainable high performance. Quality consistently beats quantity in creating lasting value for customers and sustainable results for yourself.
