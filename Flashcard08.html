Deep Dive: The Excellence Mindset includes Purpose Over Process as a core principle. Instead of asking "What am I supposed to do?" excellence asks "What outcome am I trying to create?" Understanding the why behind every activity transforms your work from a series of tasks into purposeful action. This doesn't mean ignoring process—processes matter—but it means never losing sight of the purpose those processes serve. When you connect your actions to meaningful outcomes, even routine activities become purposeful. You're not just making calls; you're creating conversations that move opportunities forward. You're not just updating your CRM; you're capturing intelligence that improves future interactions.
