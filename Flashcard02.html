What is the real definition of mediocrity?

Short Answer
Mediocrity is failing to perform to your full potential, not just performing poorly.
Deep Dive
This realization is striking because it means every time we take shortcuts or don't do our best when we have the capability, we are choosing mediocrity. Most salespeople know what they should be doing and have the capability to perform better than they do.
