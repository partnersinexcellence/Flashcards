Activity everywhere doesn't equal results. Making calls because you're supposed to isn't the same as creating value. We've confused motion with progress, attending meetings with driving outcomes, and being overwhelmed with being productive.

Help me understand
Deep Dive: Walk into most sales offices and you'll see activity everywhere—phones ringing, keyboards clicking, calendars filling with video calls, dashboards updating. Everyone appears busy, many are overwhelmed. Yet somehow, despite all this activity, results remain elusive for many. The uncomfortable truth is that being busy is not the same as being effective. Going through the motions means executing prescribed activities without genuine engagement or thoughtfulness. You can point to your activities as evidence of effort, but deep down you know something's missing. And more importantly, your results reflect it.
