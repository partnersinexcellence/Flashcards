Deep Dive: This reframes excellence from an external demand (what your manager requires or what will impress others) to an internal commitment. You pursue excellence because you care about yourself—about your potential, your growth, your professional life—enough to not settle. It's a form of self-respect and self-care. When you stay curious and keep learning, you're saying "My growth matters." When you take ownership instead of making excuses, you're saying "I'm capable of influencing my outcomes." The fuel for the excellence journey has to come from genuine care for your own development.
