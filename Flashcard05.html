How should you reframe the pursuit of excellence in terms of self-care?

Short Answer
Excellence should be reframed as an act of caring for yourself, your work, and your professional life.
Deep Dive
When you commit to performing with purpose, you're saying "My work matters enough to deserve my full attention." When you stay curious and keep learning, you're saying "My growth and development matter." This reframes excellence from an external demand to an internal commitment to care about yourself.
