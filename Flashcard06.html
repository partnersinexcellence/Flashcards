The performance gap in sales is not about tools or training but about what?

Short Answer
The performance gap is fundamentally about mindset and behaviors, not tools or training.
Deep Dive
Companies spend heavily on tools, programs, and training, yet aren't seeing results. The fundamental issue is that we're trying to solve a mindset and behavior problem with tactical solutions. Two salespeople with identical training and resources can produce dramatically different results based on how they think and the behaviors they demonstrate.
